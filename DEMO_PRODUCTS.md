## Setup Instructions

1. **In Shopify Admin**:
   - Create 5 products with the handles above
   - Upload product images from the URLs provided
   - Create two collections: "Story Book" and "Flowers and Birds"
   - Add products to their respective collections

2. **In Theme Editor**:
   - Go to Homepage → Featured Products section
   - Add 5 product blocks with the details above
   - Set button links to the product pages
   - Upload images or use image URLs

3. **For Demo Without Shopify Admin**:
   - The theme will show placeholder products from the preset
   - Update image URLs in theme settings to use Unsplash links
   - Product links will be placeholders unless real products exist
